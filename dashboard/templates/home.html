About us

We are a team participating in the 2024 NASA Space Apps Challenge, dedicated to leveraging Earth observation data to empower farmers in making informed decisions. Our project focuses on utilizing NASA datasets to tackle water-related challenges, enhance agricultural practices, and improve food security.

Our Mission

To connect farmers with critical data that helps them adapt to changing environmental conditions and optimize their agricultural practices for sustainable growth.

Our Vision

To foster a resilient agricultural community empowered by real-time data insights that enhance decision-making and productivity.

Help & Support

To use the tool, simply select the date for which you want the rainfall forecast. After selecting the date, click on the area on the map where you wish to receive the forecast. Wait for a few seconds while the forecast is generated.
To select an area, you can use the drawing tools provided in the sidebar on the map. You can draw a rectangle or a polygon by clicking on the map to create points. Once you've outlined your desired area, the rainfall forecast will be generated for that selection.
The forecast uses a color-coded system to indicate rainfall probability: green represents high probability, yellow indicates medium probability, and red shows low probability of rain.
The accuracy of the rainfall forecast depends on various factors, including the quality of the input data and the algorithms used for prediction. Generally, forecasts improve as more historical data is analyzed.
We utilize various datasets provided by NASA, including precipitation data, satellite imagery, and other relevant meteorological information to generate accurate rainfall forecasts.
